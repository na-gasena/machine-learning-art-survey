# Works Archive and Media Links

## Policy

This page points to artwork pages, exhibition archives, project pages, and publicly available media. Images are not copied into this repository; they are referenced from artist, museum, or project URLs. Check each external site's rights, terms, image changes, and hotlinking behavior before reuse.

**Link guide:** "work/official" indicates artist or producer sources; "exhibition/collection" indicates institutional records; and "video/image" marks pages where the work can be inspected online. Limits are noted when only a paper or sparse record survives.

## Update and Preservation Status

- Official pages with images or video were prioritized, but external URL persistence cannot be guaranteed.
- A paper can survive after a dataset or running work disappears. Artwork sites, runtimes, models, and datasets need separate preservation records.
- Platform-dependent archives can require login, impose region restrictions, or change URLs. They should be replaced by stable artist or institutional archives when available.

## Usage Notes

External image URLs may change. Downloading, reusing, or redistributing images requires permission or compliance with each artist's or institution's terms.
